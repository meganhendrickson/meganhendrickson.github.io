Week 1 Notes

Master Mobile UX Video

Rewire your brain
  • focus on content and core functionality
  • whatever you are doing - do mobile first - do not retrofit
  • mobile first provides the best experience across devices
  • start small and scale up
Understanding mobile users
  • observe your users and what they need
  • be there, be useful, be quick
  • don't frustrate your users - make it easy
    • fewer steps
    • reachability
    • loading times - reduce image usage, webfonts, optimize and minify css and js, gzip files
Mobile Menu Design
  • reachability - place menu at bottom
  • make it obvious
Mobile Forms
  • enlarge input fields and float labels
  • simplify information needed ask for as little as possible
  • paginate or create sections for long forms
  • enable auto complete
  • display credit cards and phone numbers in easy to read formates
  • bring up number keypad instead of keyboard when appropriate
  • always link your phone number for an easy tap and call
Mobile Advertising
  • keep them small and simple
  • larger ad revealed when clicked
  • consider video ads
  • Stop using pop-ups and pop-overs
  • content first - keep ads unobtrusive
